Croatia Luxury Travel Company

VisitCroatia.com – Tasteful Croatian Journeys is a Luxury Travel Company registered in the State of New York and based in New York City, that specializes in tailor designed, luxury travel experiences to Croatia and the surrounding countries.

Founder and President Wanda S. Radetti, with her team of Croatia specialists, create customized itineraries like a composer arranging a musical masterpiece, collecting notes about each individual traveler’s desires and needs and forming them into a harmonious journey of a lifetime. Her lyricism and expertise of Croatia is second to none. Since 2006, she has consistently been listed as Condé Nast Traveler’s World Top Travel Specialist for Croatia. In 2020, she was also recognized on Travel + Leisure’s A-List of top destination specialists. Her extensive knowledge of Croatia is honed and updated from the multiple site inspections she conducts in the country each year.

VisitCroatia.com – Tasteful Croatian Journeys travelers enjoy chauffeured transport, private guided tours, luxury accommodation, and curated cultural experiences, enhanced by real-time personalized concierge support. Our portfolio includes only the best 4 and 5 star hotels, boutique hotels, heritage properties, and private villas. Our collection of private charter options ranges from comfortable family gullets to the most luxurious yachts on the Adriatic, served by the finest captains and crew.

Tasteful Croatian Journeys trips meld scenic beauty, historical context, and gastronomic delight, highlighting artisanal culinary specialties and, for oenophiles, emerging and established world-class wineries. Harmonizing your preferences and our expertise we create a bespoke journey of relaxed elegance, including sailboat and yacht options. For heritage travelers, we facilitate local genealogical searches and coordinate ancestry excursions and family reunions.

Every customized itinerary also includes our personally vetted, detailed suggestions for sightseeing, dining, shopping, and other leisure activities. Upon arrival in Croatia, our travelers gain exclusive access to our local Tasteful Croatian Journeys Concierge who works to ensure all services are executed to perfection. Our Tasteful Croatian Journeys Concierge is at your service to provide assistance and to support with booking of additional experiences such as VIP dining reservations and wine and olive oil tastings, as well as to provide care and support that might be required due to unforeseen circumstances.

From enjoying the sweet oysters of Ston and earthy truffles of Istria, to exploring the bustling markets of Zagreb and sailing the crystal blue waters of the Adriatic Sea, Wanda and her team provide the very best in tailor-made luxury travel.
